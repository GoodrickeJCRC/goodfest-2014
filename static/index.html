Line up

This unbelievable up-and-coming pop-rock 4-piece will sucker you in with their Bastille-avec-guitars musical stylings spliced with the song-writing prowess of One Republic. Sons & Lovers have an impressive touring record playing with the likes of Kyla La Grange, Of Monsters & Men and none other than global sensation, Ellie Goulding, having also enjoyed festival slots at Reading & Leeds Festival and T in the Park.

Sons & Lovers on Facebook

Coasts are a five piece band originating from Bristol who play their own brand of tropical pop. Having formed in 2012 the band have built a loyal following through extensive touring and the successful release of their debut EP titled ‘Paradise’ in 2013.

Coasts are currently in the studio with producer Eliot James (Two Door Cinema Club, Noah & The Whale) recording their debut album due for release this summer.

A diverse group of musicians who found their feet busking on the streets of York. The Y Street Band as a 5 piece are equipped to undertake popular cover songs with a particular emphasis on rich vocal harmony.

Hitting Goodricke on June 14th

GoodFest is the biggest free annual festival hosted by the best college at the University of York: Goodricke! Originally started in 2009 as a small one-day festival, this year is the event's fifth anniversary and is set to be bigger and better than ever!

This year the free after-party is to be held in the Law & Management Atrium. The venue is going to be decked out with the best lighting and sound system to create a truly unique space for an on-campus club night. With the England World Cup match playing in the bar area, plus some fresh music from local DJs and producer Boney, this is a night you cannot miss!

The festival is open to all: students, residents and friends! So make sure Saturday 14th June is free in your diary, and don't forget to like the GoodFest page, join the Facebook event and the after-party event for instant updates for all things GoodFest!
